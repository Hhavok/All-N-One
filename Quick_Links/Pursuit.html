APB ALERT PROCEDURES - Pursuits

After a High-speed Pursuit or Amber Alert has been confirmed through the CHP's CAD system, Dispatchers must make these types of incidents their HIGHEST PRIORITY!

All High-speed Pursuits must be broadcast over the radio

  1. Prior to transmitting over the radio, the following information must be obtained:

    1. Highway/State Route, Direction, Location, i.e. off/on ramp, over/under crossing, Junctions or Interchanges.

    2. Vehicle Description, Speed and/or Weapons

  2. Select the area where the pursuit is taking place. Simul-select all channels in that area. Press and hold the RED ALERT BEEP button for 3-4 beeps. Release the button, press the transmit button and announce the following over the selected channels.


			
			"ATTENTION ALL CALTRANS PERSONNEL,
			ATTENTION ALL CALTRANS PERSONNEL...

			There is a high-speed persuit in progress in the vicinity of...
			(Specify current direction & most recent location).
			Please stop all work, return to your vehicle and fasten all safety 
			restraints immediately.

			DO NOT ATTEMPT TO PICK UP YOUR CLOSURE
			DO NOT ATTEMPT TO MOVE YOUR TRUCKS OR
			EQUIPMENT UNTIL FURTHER NOTICE."

	During the pursuit, check the CHP Log frequently for any updates and 
	transmit all current information over the selected channels.Please 
	follow Step #1 each and every time there is an update to the Log. 
	After the pursuit has ended, once again, press & hold RED ALERT BEEP
	button for 3-4 beeps and transmit the following:

			"ATTENTION ALL CALTRANS PERSONNEL,
			 ATTENTION ALL CALTRANS PERSONNEL...

			The high-speed pursuit has ended, you may return to 
			your normal closure activity."
